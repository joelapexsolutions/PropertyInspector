Property Viewing Guide

Expert tips to maximize your property viewings

Before the Viewing

  • Research the area crime statistics and property prices
  • Check municipal rates and levies
  • Prepare questions about property history
  • Charge your phone for photos
  • Download Property Inspector app

During the Viewing

  • Test all taps, lights, and appliances
  • Check for water damage and damp
  • Inspect roof condition (if accessible)
  • Test water pressure and drainage
  • Document everything with photos

Questions to Ask

  • Why is the property being sold?
  • Age of electrical and plumbing systems?
  • Any building work without approval?
  • Average monthly utility costs?
  • Any known defects or issues?

Red Flags to Watch

  • Fresh paint covering wall cracks
  • Musty smells or visible mold
  • Sloping or uneven floors
  • Electrical hazards or old wiring
  • Rushed viewings or pressure tactics
