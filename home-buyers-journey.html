10 Steps to Buying with Confidence

Your Complete Home Buying Journey

Most buyers miss critical steps that cost R50,000–R200,000 after moving in. Follow this roadmap to inspect smart, negotiate hard, and buy with total confidence.

Did you know? 70% of South African homeowners face unexpected repair costs within their first year of ownership, often exceeding R50,000. These surprises stem from skipping crucial steps in the buying process. This guide — and the Home Buyers Guide SA app — ensures you know exactly what to look for before you sign.

Step-by-Step Breakdown

Everything you need to know at each stage of the process

1

Know Your Budget

Get pre-approved for a home loan so you know your maximum limit before you start viewing. Pre-approval takes 1–3 days and shows sellers you're a serious buyer.

2

Calculate Your True Costs

Your loan is only part of it. Transfer duties, attorney fees, and bond registration typically add R30,000–R80,000+. Use our free calculator to see the full picture.

Free Cost Calculator
3

Search Smart

Browse Property24 and Private Property within your budget. Shortlist 5–10 properties that meet your must-haves: location, size, bedrooms, and key features.

4

Research the Property

Before viewing, check market value, crime stats, amenities, and area trends. Standard Bank's free Property Guide and Lightstone reports help you spot overpriced listings.

5

Prepare Like a Pro

Download the Home Buyers Guide SA app, add your shortlisted properties, and review the room-by-room checklist before every viewing. Preparation prevents costly oversights.

6

View with Confidence

Use the app to assess every room and outdoor space. Snap photos of concerns and get free opinions from professionals before committing. Spot it. Snap it. Know it.

7

Compare and Decide

Review your assessment reports side-by-side. Compare scores, repair costs, and monthly expenses across properties to remove emotion and make a data-driven decision.

8

Negotiate Strategically

Use your documented repair estimates to negotiate a lower price or request seller repairs before transfer. Evidence-backed negotiation gives you real leverage.

9

Secure Your Financing

Formally apply for your home loan once your offer is accepted. Submit all documents promptly — ID, bank statements, proof of income. Expect approval within 1–4 weeks.

10

Move In and Maintain

After transfer (8–12 weeks), do a final walk-through with the app to create a baseline report. Build a maintenance plan for your first 1–3 years so nothing catches you off guard.

Why This Process Matters

Following these 10 steps transforms your home buying experience from stressful and uncertain to confident and informed. You'll avoid the shock of discovering R50,000+ in unexpected repairs after moving in, negotiate better prices based on actual property conditions, and maintain the joy of homeownership instead of experiencing buyer's remorse. Knowledge is your best investment protection.
