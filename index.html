Guided Property Viewing Checklist

One complete checklist covering four categories — location, exterior, interior, and other features. Add custom items for anything specific to the property, like a pool, solar system, or extra room. Takes 45–90 minutes and saves automatically as you go.

Learn What to Look For

Built-in tooltips explain exactly what to inspect — from identifying serious cracks to spotting covered-up water damage. Learn as you conduct viewings.

Document Red Flags

Capture and organise photos by room during viewings. Build visual evidence for negotiations, insurance, or professional consultations. Photos are embedded in reports.

Smart Scoring System

Rate each aspect as Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor. Intelligent weighting prioritises critical items like structural integrity. Get an overall condition rating and full breakdown.

Professional PDF Reports

Generate detailed reports with photos, scores, issue summaries, and recommendations. Share with family, contractors, or use for price negotiations.

Home Buying Cost Calculator

Calculate accurate monthly costs including bond repayments, transfer duties, attorney fees, rates, and utilities. Budget for the true cost of ownership.

Negotiation Power

Get expert negotiation tips based on issues found. Use documented red flags to justify price reductions. Know when to walk away vs. negotiate repairs.

Complete Privacy

All data stored locally on your device. No cloud storage, no data sharing. Your property viewing information remains completely private and secure.
